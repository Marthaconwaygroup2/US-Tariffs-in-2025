Long-term outlook and system-level implications

Economic Outlook

Tariffs represent a significant economic shock with 0.6% persistent GDP reduction in the US, and 0.3-1.5% reductions for major trading partners, potentially triggering recession in 63% of surveyed scenarios.

-1.1pp US GDP growth impact 2025

Supply Chain Reconfiguration

The tariff regime is accelerating a bifurcated global trading system with parallel US-centric and China-centric supply chains. Friend-shoring outpacing reshoring 4:1 with Mexico, Vietnam, Thailand as key beneficiaries.

Geopolitical Shifts

Accelerated formation of regional trading blocs and deepening China-EU economic ties represent long-term structural shifts. 2025 tariffs appear to be catalyzing rather than initiating trade realignments already underway.
