Reshoring vs. Friendshoring Trends

Reshoring

  • ▪ Advantages: Greater control, IP protection, shortened lead times
  • ▪ Challenges: 15-40% higher production costs, labor shortages
  • ▪ Adoption: 28% of surveyed companies with active plans (vs. 12% in 2023)
  • ▪ Sectors: Semiconductors, pharmaceuticals, critical minerals

Friendshoring

  • ▪ Advantages: Cost efficiency, diversification without drastic changes
  • ▪ Challenges: Infrastructure gaps, skill shortages in new locations
  • ▪ Adoption: 65% of companies moving production to allies (CNBC survey)
  • ▪ Key destinations: Mexico, Vietnam, Malaysia, India, Poland

$3B additional investment in existing facilities

Completion: Q4 2025

Key Strategic Insight

Companies are pursuing dual strategies: targeted reshoring for critical components and friendshoring to allies for cost efficiency. These shifts will permanently alter global supply chain architectures, with an increasing emphasis on supply chain resilience over pure cost optimization.
