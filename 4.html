Tariff Policy Overview

The "Liberation Day" tariffs announced April 2, 2025

Base Tariff

Minimum 10% tariff on all imports to the U.S., with significantly higher rates for specific countries

Implementation

10% tariffs effective April 5, 2025; higher country-specific rates implemented April 9, 2025

Exceptions

Certain semiconductors, pharmaceuticals, energy resources, and critical minerals exempt from tariffs
